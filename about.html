ABOUT US

AK Martial Arts and Fitness Academy was founded by Shihan Ashish Kumar in the year 2017.This esteemed institute has now grown into 3 branches with 200+ students across all the branches.

Our academy collaborates with 7 prestigious schools and 30 apartments/villas to provide Karate and self-defense training to 2000+ children as part of their curriculum.

  • The team consists of well-trained and experienced instructors who are dedicated to providing quality training to their students.
  • Open for children above 4 years and for adults.
  • Focus on overall development in terms of Physical fitness, Strength, Flexibility and Discipline.
Shihan Ashish Kumar
Founder and Technical Director of AK Martial Arts and Fitness Academy. Founder and President of AK Shito-Ryu Karate Do Sports Association-India.
